Our Story

Founded in 2008, Racchi Landscaping is a family-owned business serving Vancouver and the surrounding areas. What began as a small operation with just two employees has grown into a trusted name in residential and commercial landscaping.

Our team takes pride in every project we undertake, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to create outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

We believe in sustainable landscaping practices that enhance your property while respecting the natural environment.
